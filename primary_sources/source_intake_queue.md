## 1. HERO PRIORITY — The Deed Itself

- [ ] **1682 Joane Greene Consent — Pawtuxet Deed** 
    - **Status:** 🟢 PROVEN (was PROBABLE — witnesses restored)

These deeds prove the world that made her 1682 consent necessary.

- [ ] **1672 Anashuecot Deed — Fones Record pp.166-169**

    - **Why Joane:** 7 signers all recorded as "his marke" — even sachem squas. Proves scribal construct that may hide women. Direct comparator for Joane's gendered recording.

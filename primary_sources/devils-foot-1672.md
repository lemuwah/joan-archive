## Why it matters
This page names **Womphagee, Ompaniat, Tyecumsha, and Mammo[osk]** as a
brothers/sons consent group giving possession to John Green — the same structural
group named in the 1672 Anashuecot deed body as **Wampkegge & Ompamiatt
(brothers)** and **Seecomp, Tyecuecsha & Nammeash (sons)** (see
[`1672-fones.md`](/primary/1672-fones/)).

MANIFEST logs this as **proving Costa nasalization (w- ↔ n-/m-) links the same
consent group** across the two spellings — i.e., this is being used as internal,
same-archive evidence that the name variants refer to the same people, not
independent evidence from an outside source. Read it as a strong internal
consistency check, not as a third-party corroboration.
